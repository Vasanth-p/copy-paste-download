Information

Paste Image to Download is a utility website made by Tamilselvan V for downloading images in your clipboard.

This tool was made for those moments where you take a screenshot and it is directly copied to the clipboard.

Instead of going to MS Paint, pasting the image, choosing a location, and saving itâ€¦ You can just paste it on this website.

The pasted images are processed and downloaded locally on your device. No data is ever sent.

Additionally, you can use the "Select Folder" feature to choose a folder on your device where the images will be saved automatically. This is especially useful for managing multiple images or organizing them into specific folders.

This is an open-source project, check out the source code.
